Research Overview

My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, healthcare informatics, and radiation oncology. I lead innovative projects that leverage cutting-edge technology to improve cancer care delivery, enhance clinical decision-making, and advance precision medicine approaches.

Our team collaborates with leading institutions and industry partners to develop AI-powered solutions that address real-world clinical challenges. From large language models for radiation oncology to computer vision for cancer detection, we're pushing the boundaries of what's possible in healthcare technology.

Current research areas include:

  • AI and Machine Learning in Radiation Oncology
  • Clinical Decision Support Systems
  • Healthcare Data Interoperability
  • Digital Health for Underserved Communities
  • Predictive Analytics for Treatment Outcomes
